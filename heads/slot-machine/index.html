About

The project is aptly named ‘Brainhead,’ a fusion of ‘brain’ and ‘head,’ signifying that each piece is a direct expression from the artist’s mind—art as a manifestation of thought.

The incorporation of a slot-machine element subtly comments on the engrossing nature of these games, hinting at the intense focus they demand from players.
